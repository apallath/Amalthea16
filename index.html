About Amalthea '16

The need for a better life has always driven man to inexplicable heights, compelling him to find motivation in the shallowest of depths and hope in the darkest of corners. As we are advancing further down the road of development, the challenges are only becoming more unfathomable and demanding. The need for creative means of problem solving has increased exponentially in the last decade.

The objective of Amalthea '16 is to bring out the simple correspondence between technology and development based on the theme 'Technologizing Challenges' and spread knowledge - the ultimate aim of Amalthea.

Amalthea '16 will feature a Conclave on the same theme for the speakers to share their generation of ideas , an Exhibition on 'Expediting Development Through Tech and Innovation', a Symposium on ‘Technologies in Defense and Internal Security’ and many other engaging events.

Explore Amalthea

Conclave
Theme: 'Technologizing Challenges'

Conclave invites thinkers, ideators, and leaders in industry and academia to share their experiences, expertise and research with aspiring individuals.

Exhibition
Theme: 'Expediting Development Through Tech and Innovation'

Exhibition provides a platform to showcase technologies and products to the inquisitive and interested public that includes students, designers, engineers, academicians, entrepreneurs, and investors.

Symposium
Theme: 'Technologies in Defense and Internal Security'

Symposium provides a platform for research scholars and technology enthusiasts, with common interests and passions, to share and discuss their research.
